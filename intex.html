Our Story

Over a Decade of
Textile Heritage

Nitu Fashion Private Limited is a textile trading company incorporated in 2008 in West Bengal. With over 15 years of operational excellence, we connect manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers through a robust and reliable supply chain.

Founded with a commitment to quality and trust, we have built lasting relationships across the Indian textile ecosystem. Our expertise spans sourcing premium fabrics, dress materials, and speciality apparel — delivered with precision and care.

Headquartered in West Bengal, we are the go-to distribution partner for the eastern region, with a steadily growing footprint across all of India.

"Trusted by traders across India — woven into the fabric of the nation's textile trade since 2008."
